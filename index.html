Dairy Plant Improvement Methodology⌄

The improvement methodology combines floor-level observation, KRA review, daily meetings, and Gemba-based action tracking to create measurable operational improvement in dairy plants.

1
Shop-floor observation and detailed line walkthrough.
2
Review of production, quality, and dispatch records.
3
Interaction and discussion with department heads and key personnel.
4
Identification of deviations, operational gaps, and root causes.
5
Daily Gemba meetings to resolve issues and improve inter-department coordination.
6
Preparation of action plans with responsibilities and timelines.
7
Follow-up review of corrective actions and performance improvement.
